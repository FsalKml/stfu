Each NFT entitles its holders for a 10% off from our list of selected boutiques hotels namely in Langkawi Island, Malaysia as our pilot location (valid for 1 year from date of mint). From one digital nomad to the other, we take into consideration our extensive past experience with working with worldwide digital nomads addressing issues such as fast & stable internet connection, safety, cultural immersion, environmental care, low cost of living, co-working spaces etc. Plus that with the free usage of coworking redeemable together with the night(s) stayed.
